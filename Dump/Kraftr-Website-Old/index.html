    Story

    Kraftr is a journey of sustainable innovation, where a diverse team of artisans in Manipal transforms vintage surplus into exquisite, eco-conscious fashion.

    Take a Trip
    The Kraftr Crew

    A team of diverse and passionate individuals, hailing from various corners of India, has converged in the cultural melting pot of Manipal to form the heart and soul of Kraftr. The Kraftr Crew represents a harmonious fusion of artistry, craftsmanship, and an unwavering commitment to eco-consciousness. Together, they combine their unique talents and shared dedication to bring the vision of sustainable, handmade, and elegant fashion to life.
